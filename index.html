Historic Milestones

1965 - First Spacewalk

Alexei Leonov performed the first-ever spacewalk, spending 12 minutes outside his Voskhod 2 spacecraft, paving the way for future extravehicular activities.

1977 - Voyager Golden Record

NASA launched Voyager 1 and 2 with golden records containing sounds and images from Earth, intended as a message to potential extraterrestrial life.

1995 - First Exoplanet Discovery

Astronomers discovered 51 Pegasi b, the first confirmed exoplanet orbiting a sun-like star, revolutionizing our understanding of planetary systems.

2014 - Rosetta's Comet Landing

The Philae lander became the first spacecraft to successfully land on a comet, providing unprecedented data about these ancient celestial objects.
